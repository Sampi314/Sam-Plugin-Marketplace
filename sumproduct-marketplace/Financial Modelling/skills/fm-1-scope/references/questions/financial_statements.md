# Financial Statement Questions (Groups C-G)

> Use these when the model includes a P&L, Balance Sheet, or Cash Flow Statement (3-statement, corporate finance, project finance models).

## Group C — Revenue (ask per stream identified in Round 1)
- What drives volume? What drives price?
- Any contractual escalation or inflation linkage?
- Ramp-up period before full capacity?
- Seasonality? (If monthly model)

## Group D — Costs
- Main cost categories? Which are fixed vs variable?
- Any costs linked directly to revenue (royalties, commissions)?
- Staff costs — headcount-driven? Annual raises?

## Group E — Capital & Depreciation
- What CapEx items? Cost, timing, useful life?
- Depreciation method? (Suggest: "Straight-line is standard. Diminishing value or units-of-production only if required by accounting standards or the asset type warrants it.")
- Maintenance/lifecycle CapEx?

## Group F — Funding & Debt
- Equity and/or debt? How is the project capitalised?
- Debt terms — even approximate: amount, rate, term, repayment shape?
- Any covenants or lock-up tests?

## Group G — Working Capital & Tax
- Receivable, payable, inventory days?
- Corporate tax rate? Tax losses?
- Other taxes relevant to the sector?
