# Commission / Incentive Calculation Questions

- What is the commission structure? (flat %, tiered, threshold-based, accelerators?)
- How many salespeople / roles / territories?
- What triggers a commission? (booking, invoicing, cash receipt?)
- Are there clawback provisions? (e.g., customer cancels within X months)
- Caps or floors on commission?
- Split commissions between team members?
- How often is commission paid? (monthly, quarterly, annually?)
- Are there non-revenue triggers? (new logos, renewals, upsells?)
