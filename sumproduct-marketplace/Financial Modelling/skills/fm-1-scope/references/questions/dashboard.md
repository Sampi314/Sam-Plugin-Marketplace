# Dashboard / Reporting Focus Questions

- Who is the primary audience? (board, management, investors, regulators?)
- What KPIs matter most? How many?
- Comparative data needed? (prior period, budget, forecast?)
- Drill-down capability or summary only?
- Charts — what types? (waterfall, trend, variance, gauge?)
- Refresh frequency? (live-linked or periodic snapshot?)
- Print-ready or screen-only?
