# Detailed Operational / Component-Level Questions

- What level of detail is needed? (per asset, per mine block, per production line, per store?)
- How many individual units/components?
- What are the key physical drivers? (tonnes, units, hours, headcount?)
- Is there a ramp-up or phase-in schedule per component?
- Maintenance schedules per component?
- Capacity constraints or bottleneck modelling?
- Do components interact? (e.g., mine feeds processing plant, plant has separate capacity)
- Decommissioning or end-of-life provisioning per component?
