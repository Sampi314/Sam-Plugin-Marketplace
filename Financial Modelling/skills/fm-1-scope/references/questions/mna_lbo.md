# M&A / LBO Questions

- Is this buy-side or sell-side?
- Purchase price and consideration structure? (cash, stock, earnout?)
- Sources and uses of funds?
- Synergies — what type and timing? (cost savings, revenue synergies, one-off integration costs?)
- Pro-forma balance sheet needed?
- Goodwill and purchase price allocation?
- For LBO: leverage targets, debt tranches, management equity rollover?
- Exit assumptions? (holding period, exit multiple)
