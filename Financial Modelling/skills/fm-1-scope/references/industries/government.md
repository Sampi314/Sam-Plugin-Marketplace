# Government & NFP

## Typical Revenue/Funding Drivers
- Grant funding (tranche-based, milestone-linked)
- Government appropriation (annual budget allocation)
- User fees / charges
- Cost recovery mechanisms
- Donations / fundraising

## Cost Drivers to Suggest
- Staff costs (EBA/award rates, superannuation, leave provisions)
- Program delivery costs
- Compliance and reporting costs
- Overhead allocation

## Key Considerations
- **Funding conditions** — acquittal requirements, eligible expenditure
- **Cash vs accrual** — government often reports on cash basis
- **Forward estimates** — 3-4 year horizon typical
- **Efficiency dividends** — mandated cost reductions
